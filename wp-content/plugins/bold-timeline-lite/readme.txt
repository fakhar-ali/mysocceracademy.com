== Description ==

**[Bold Timeline Lite](https://bold-timeline.bold-themes.com/lite-main-demo/) – WordPress Timeline Plugin**

[Bold Timeline Lite](https://bold-timeline.bold-themes.com/lite-main-demo/) is a WordPress timeline plugin that lets you show, easily, any number of events in, for example, company history, author biography, history events, life stories, work experience, step-by-step guide, events timeline or other.

**[Bold Timeline Lite](https://bold-timeline.bold-themes.com/lite-main-demo/) is super easy to use!**

Our goal was to make it real easy for anyone to use by eliminating all the hassle that comes from overly complex settings. It has [5 predefined styles](https://bold-timeline.bold-themes.com/lite-main-demo/) ready for you. Choose your style, then import your own text and images and your timeline is done. In addition, you have the option to change colors, fonts and icons to reflect your particular taste and style.

Our [predefined styles](https://bold-timeline.bold-themes.com/lite-main-demo/):

* [Classic](https://bold-timeline.bold-themes.com/lite-main-demo/classic-demo/)
* [Retro](https://bold-timeline.bold-themes.com/lite-main-demo/retro-demo/)
* [Clean](https://bold-timeline.bold-themes.com/lite-main-demo/clean-demo/)
* [Travel](https://bold-timeline.bold-themes.com/lite-main-demo/travel-demo/)
* [CV](https://bold-timeline.bold-themes.com/lite-main-demo/cv-demo/)

This timeline has its own shortcode builder which makes it easy to use with any WordPress page builder. Bold Timeline Lite for WordPress is simple and easy to use, can be used to show any sequence of events or steps in several different ways. It is responsive and will look good on any screen size.
